About Us

Now, the modern world is more connected and online than ever before. The latest technological developments are revolutionizing the planet by increasing the ease and accessibility of services for people. At Ideathon 2021, we invite all developers and hackathon enthusiasts to come up with fantastic ideas for changing the world. Teams with the most innovative ideas and fantastic pitches will win exciting prizes. It is open to all developers who have an eye for design and a passion to innovate. Participants can hack as a team of up to 5 participants. Take advantage of this opportunity to learn with other teams, showcase your talent, and compete for prizes!

Rules

  • Team Size: 1 3 members
  • Domain: Must be one of the 8 domains provided by us.
  • Rounds: ~There will be 2 Rounds:
    • Round 1: PPT Submission The participant teams will send their ppts to the Ideathon Registration Form by Wednesday 8th December 2021 and the top 10 teams will be selected from them on the basis of idea, presentation and innovation.
    • Round 2: Pitching Each team will get 10 minutes to present their idea on 11th - 13th December 2021 in front of a panel of judges and the winners will be selected on the basis of innovation, clarity of pitch and potential for the idea.
  • Submission Guidelines:
  • Only one PowerPoint presentation is allowed for each team and is to be used for both the rounds. The first slide must contain the team name, product name, participant names, college/company and domain of product. The presentation must not exceed 10 slides, and will be judged on the basis of:
  • Problem and Statement
  • Solution
  • Architecture Diagram
  • Business Model
  • Market
  • Execution Plan
  • Tech Stack
  • Future Scope
  • Future Scope

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the PPT need to follow a particular format?

Please refer the sample PPT and submission guidelines in the rules section.

How many members can take part in a team?

Maximum 5 people are allowed in a team.

Do we need to pay any registration fee for the Ideathon?

There is no registration fee for Ideathon 21.

Can we use a video instead of pitching the idea live?

Unless you are facing serious technical issues, we will require live presentation in the final round.

Can the participants be working professionals or non-students?

Yes, we welcome participants from every field and qualification. There are no criteria applied regarding team members.

PICT IEEE Student Branch
OPEN INNOVATION
This track welcomes ideas from participants solving problems in any domain of their choice. Enabling the community to broaden the scope of employment opportunities, remote surveillance, social benefits or any domain will be welcome. May the most innovative ideas win!
HEALTHCARE
The COVID-19 pandemic has starved the world of healthcare services, and has worsened the gap in accessibility tremendously. Many people around the world are limited in their access to necessary healthcare services, thereby increasing their risk of poor health outcomes and serious complications. This track is to encourage innovative healthcare solutions including diagnostic techniques, healthcare chat bots, products using connected technologies like wearables and smartphones, mental healthcare assistance or other solutions.
EDUCATION
The recent COVID-19 pandemic has brought a dramatic shift in the way we perceive education. Educational institutions have been challenged to transform their traditional teaching patterns to accommodate the remote scenario. In this track we encourage ideas that will revolutionize the field of remote learning, smart classrooms and interactive education.
INTERNET OF THINGS
IoT has emerged as one of the most important technologies of the twenty-first century in recent years. Now that we can connect everyday objects to the internet via embedded devices. Physical things can share and collect data with minimal human interaction thanks to low-cost computers, the cloud, big data, analytics, and mobile technologies. Digital systems can record, monitor, and alter each interaction between connected entities in this hyperconnected environment. The physical world collides with the digital worldand they work together.
BANKING & FINTECH
In this track we are looking for ideas including by not limited to the adoption of mobile payment technologies, insurance fraud detection systems and financial management products for professionals. In short, we encourage products that will solve existing challenges in the banking sector, with the help of the latest technologies.
AGRICULTURE
India is a global agricultural powerhouse. From Kashmir to Tamil Nadu, farmers in India are some of the most neglected population segments with inadequate access to technology, financial resources and education. This track welcomes all ideas aiming to disrupt agriculture in the modern world, from remote crop monitoring solutions to effective yield estimation. We encourage solutions that could help farmers increase their profits, reduce their losses or that simply integrate technology with their daily activities while being cost-friendly and accessible.
ENVIRONMENT & SUSTAINABILITY
The climate is the most important concern of this decade, as the consequences of our harmful activities are showing their effects on the Earth. From pollution detecting technologies to sustainable products, this track welcomes innovative solutions that will tackle climate change and create a greener world.
AUGMENTED/ VIRTUAL REALITY
Augmented reality (AR) will alter everything from travel to real estate, healthcare to education, thanks to breakthroughs in computer vision, AI, and cloud computing.
